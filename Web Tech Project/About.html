What does AutoMate stand for?

But what about the survey?

Every company conducts a survey once or twice in a year so that they get the public opinion which helps them for their upcoming projects.

Our survey helps us understand the type, number, make and model of cars that are most frequently bought. It also helps us understandd the car market and demand of customers. The survey helps us to calculate the number of cars currently on the road and an average number of cars owned per household.
